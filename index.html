Engineering Entrance Exams in India

Engineering entrance exams in India are the gateway to some of the most prestigious engineering institutions in the country. These exams are conducted at national, state, and institutional levels, offering aspiring engineers a chance to pursue undergraduate and postgraduate courses in fields such as computer science, civil engineering, mechanical engineering, and more.

The Joint Entrance Examination (JEE) is the most prominent national-level exam, comprising JEE Main and JEE Advanced, which grant admission to NITs, IIITs, and IITs. Additionally, exams like BITSAT, VITEEE, and SRMJEEE are popular for admissions into private universities like BITS Pilani, VIT, and SRM.
